LearnTech Innovation

Center for Engineering Education Technologies (CLET) collaborates with individuals, academic departments, academic support units to foster capacity and community around teaching, to promote an institutional culture that values effective teaching and meaningful learning. Our vision is to inspire teaching excellence, innovation, and investigation.

Focus Areas:

  • Creating a narrated powerpoint presentation
  • Synchronous and asynchronous online learning
  • Transitioning to Early e learning Readiness Videos(ELRV)
  • Virtual reality
  • Hypothesis
  • Data visualization
  • Concept mapping tools

Student Centric

  • Implementation of experiential learning
  • Implementation of outcome based education
